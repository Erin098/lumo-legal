Privacy Policy

Lumo — a warm reading companion · Last updated: July 2026

Lumo is designed so that your reading life stays on your device. This policy explains what information the app handles and what never leaves your phone.

What we store — and where

  • Reading data (books you open, progress, streaks, saved quotes, vocabulary, reading level, goals) is stored locally on your device only. We do not run accounts on our servers, and we cannot see this data.
  • Profile details you enter (a display name, language preferences) are also stored locally on your device.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose Sign in with Apple, authentication is handled by Apple. The app receives at most your name and email (or Apple's private relay address) and keeps them on your device to label your profile. They are not uploaded to us.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising, and no advertising SDKs.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information — we don't hold it in the first place.

Children

Lumo is made for families and is safe for children to read with a grown-up. The library contains classic, public-domain books. The app shows no ads, has no social features or chat, and collects no personal data on our servers.

Notifications

Reading reminders are optional and generated locally on your device. You can turn them off anytime in the app or in system settings.

Deleting your data

Because everything lives on your device, deleting the app deletes your data. You can also sign out or reset within the app.

Changes

If a future version of Lumo changes how data is handled (for example, optional cloud sync), we will update this policy and note it in the app before those features are enabled.

Contact

隱私政策

Lumo — 溫暖的閱讀夥伴 · 最後更新:2026 年 7 月

Lumo 的設計原則是:你的閱讀資料留在你的裝置上。本政策說明 App 會處理哪些資訊,以及哪些資訊永遠不會離開你的手機。

我們儲存什麼、存在哪裡

  • 閱讀資料(開啟的書、進度、連續天數、收藏的句子、生字本、閱讀程度、目標)只儲存在你的裝置上。我們的伺服器沒有帳號系統,也看不到這些資料。
  • 你輸入的個人資料(暱稱、語言偏好)同樣只存在裝置上。

使用 Apple 登入

若你選擇「使用 Apple 登入」,驗證由 Apple 處理。App 最多只會取得你的姓名與電子郵件(或 Apple 的私密轉寄地址),並保存在你的裝置上作為個人檔案顯示,不會上傳給我們。

我們不做的事

  • 沒有廣告,也沒有廣告 SDK。
  • 沒有第三方分析或追蹤。
  • 我們不出售、出租或分享個人資料 —— 我們本來就不持有這些資料。

兒童

Lumo 為家庭設計,適合孩子在大人陪伴下閱讀。書庫皆為經典公版書。App 沒有廣告、沒有社交或聊天功能,伺服器也不蒐集任何個人資料。

通知

閱讀提醒為選用功能,由裝置本地產生。你可以隨時在 App 內或系統設定中關閉。

刪除資料

因為所有資料都在你的裝置上,刪除 App 即刪除資料。你也可以在 App 內登出或重設。

政策變更

若未來版本改變資料處理方式(例如選用的雲端同步),我們會先更新本政策,並在功能啟用前於 App 內告知。

聯絡我們

有任何問題,請來信 erin.wu119@outlook.com。
