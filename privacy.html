Privacy Policy

Lumo — a warm reading companion · Last updated: July 2026

Lumo is built to be calm, safe, and privacy-respecting — especially for children reading with their families. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can delete it. We do not sell your personal information, we do not show third-party ads, and we do not use your data for behavioral advertising or cross-app tracking.

What we collect

  • On your device (default). Your reading progress, streaks, saved words and quotes, reading level, goals, and preferences are stored on your device. If you don't sign in, this stays on your device.
  • Account — Sign in with Apple (optional). If you sign in, Apple gives us a stable, anonymous user identifier and — only if you agree — your name and email (you may use Apple's "Hide My Email"). We store this on our servers to create your account and sync your library and progress across your devices.
  • AI Reading Adapter. When you use the adapter, the text you paste or upload is sent to our servers and to our AI processing provider (Google Gemini) solely to generate your adaptation. Results are cached on your device. This text is used only to provide the feature — never for advertising, and not to train AI models.
  • Purchases. Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation that a subscription is active; we never receive your card or payment details.
  • Minimal technical logs to keep the service running and secure. No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

How we use it

To provide and sync your reading experience, deliver the AI adapter, remember your progress and rewards, process subscriptions through Apple, and keep the service secure. That's all.

Where data is stored & who processes it

Account and sync data are stored on Amazon Web Services (United States). Our sub-processors are: Amazon Web Services (hosting), Google (Gemini AI processing for the adapter), and Apple (Sign in with Apple and App Store payments). We share data with them only as needed to run the service.

Children

Lumo is intended for children to use together with a parent or guardian. We collect the minimum needed, use a nickname only (no photos, no real-name requirement), show no ads, and never sell personal data. Contact us to remove a child's information.

Notifications

Reading reminders are optional and generated locally on your device. Turn them off anytime in the app or in system settings.

Retention & deleting your data

  • Deleting the app removes the data stored on your device.
  • To delete your account and all associated server data, email erin.wu119@outlook.com from your account address (or tell us your account details). We delete it within 30 days and confirm when done.
  • We keep account data only while your account is active, plus a short period required for security and legal obligations.

Changes

If we make material changes, we'll update this page and the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email erin.wu119@outlook.com.

隱私政策

Lumo — 溫暖的閱讀夥伴 · 最後更新:2026 年 7 月

Lumo 力求安靜、安全、尊重隱私 —— 尤其為親子共讀而設計。本政策說明我們收集什麼、為什麼收集,以及你如何刪除。我們不出售你的個人資料,不顯示第三方廣告,也不將你的資料用於行為廣告或跨 App 追蹤。

我們收集什麼

  • 裝置本地(預設)。 你的閱讀進度、連續天數、收藏的生字與句子、閱讀程度、目標與偏好都存在你的裝置上。若你不登入,這些資料就只留在裝置上。
  • 帳號 —— 使用 Apple 登入(選用)。 若你登入,Apple 會提供一個穩定的匿名識別碼,以及(僅在你同意時)你的姓名與電子郵件(可使用 Apple 的「隱藏我的電子郵件」)。我們會將這些存在伺服器上,用來建立帳號並在你的多個裝置間同步書庫與進度。
  • AI 閱讀改寫器。 當你使用改寫器時,你貼上或上傳的文字會傳送到我們的伺服器,以及我們的 AI 處理供應商(Google Gemini),僅用於生成改寫結果。結果會快取在你的裝置上。這些文字只用於提供此功能 —— 絕不用於廣告,也不用於訓練 AI 模型。
  • 購買。 訂閱由 Apple 處理。我們只會收到「訂閱有效」的確認,絕不會取得你的卡號或付款細節。
  • 最少量的技術日誌,用於維持服務運作與安全。沒有任何第三方廣告或追蹤 SDK。

我們如何使用

用於提供並同步你的閱讀體驗、提供 AI 改寫器、記住你的進度與獎勵、透過 Apple 處理訂閱,以及維持服務安全。僅此而已。

資料存放在哪、由誰處理

帳號與同步資料存放於 Amazon Web Services(美國)。我們的次處理者為:Amazon Web Services(主機代管)、Google(改寫器的 Gemini AI 處理)、Apple(Apple 登入與 App Store 付款)。我們僅在提供服務所必需的範圍內與他們共享資料。

兒童

Lumo 設計為孩子在家長或監護人陪同下使用。我們只收集最少必要資料,僅使用暱稱(不需照片、不需真實姓名),不顯示廣告,也絕不出售個人資料。若需移除孩子的資料,請與我們聯絡。

通知

閱讀提醒為選用功能,由裝置本地產生。你可隨時在 App 內或系統設定中關閉。

保留與刪除資料

  • 刪除 App 即刪除存在裝置上的資料。
  • 若要刪除帳號及所有相關的伺服器資料,請用你的帳號信箱來信 erin.wu119@outlook.com(或告知我們你的帳號資訊)。我們會在 30 天內刪除並回覆確認。
  • 我們僅在你的帳號有效期間保留帳號資料,另加一段為安全與法律義務所需的短暫期間。

政策變更

若有重大變更,我們會更新本頁與上方日期。
