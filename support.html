Contact us

For any question, bug report, suggestion, or a request to delete your data, email info@sableus.com. We usually reply within a few days.

Common questions

Where is my data? Your reading progress, streaks, and saved words are stored on your device. If you sign in with Apple, your library and progress also sync to your account on our servers. Deleting the app removes the on-device data; to delete your account and server data, email us.

Are the books free? Most of the library is free. Some titles and features (like the full AI Reading Adapter allowance) are part of an optional Premium subscription.

What is the AI Reading Adapter? You can paste or upload your own text and Lumo will translate and adapt it to a reading level. That text is sent securely to our AI provider only to create your result — never for ads.

How do reminders work? Reading reminders are optional local notifications. Turn them on or off in Profile → Reading goal, or in iOS Settings.

常見問題

我的資料存在哪裡? 你的閱讀進度、連續天數、生字存在裝置上。若你使用 Apple 登入,書庫與進度也會同步到我們伺服器上的帳號。刪除 App 會移除裝置上的資料;若要刪除帳號與伺服器資料,請來信我們。

書是免費的嗎? 書庫大部分免費。部分書籍與功能(例如完整的 AI 閱讀改寫器額度)屬於選用的 Premium 訂閱。

AI 閱讀改寫器是什麼? 你可以貼上或上傳自己的文字,Lumo 會把它翻譯並改寫成某個閱讀程度。該文字會安全地傳送給我們的 AI 供應商,僅用於生成你的結果,絕不用於廣告。

提醒怎麼運作? 閱讀提醒是選用的本地通知,可在「個人 → 閱讀目標」或 iOS 設定中開關。
