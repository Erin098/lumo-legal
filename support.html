Common questions

Where is my data? Everything (progress, streaks, saved words) is stored on your device. Deleting the app deletes your data.

The books are free? Yes — Lumo's library is built from classic public-domain works, retold at multiple reading levels.

How do reminders work? Reading reminders are optional local notifications. Turn them on or off in Profile → Reading goal, or in iOS Settings.

常見問題

我的資料存在哪裡? 所有資料(進度、連續天數、生字)都存在你的裝置上;刪除 App 即刪除資料。

書是免費的嗎? 是 —— Lumo 書庫由經典公版書組成,並改寫成多個閱讀程度。

提醒怎麼運作? 閱讀提醒是選用的本地通知,可在「個人 → 閱讀目標」或 iOS 設定中開關。
